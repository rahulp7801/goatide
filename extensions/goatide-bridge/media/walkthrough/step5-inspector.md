The **Graph Inspector** lets you explore your project's rationale graph as an interactive, time-travelable visualization. Every ConstraintNode, DecisionNode, ContractNode, OpenQuestion, and Attempt is rendered as a node; edges show how they relate.

Use the **timeline slider** to travel to any recorded snapshot in your graph history. See which nodes existed at each point in time and how the graph evolved — useful for understanding why a file has its current shape even across months of changes.

In multi-root workspaces, the **Cross-Repo Graph** command stitches together the graphs of all workspace folders into a single view (`goatide.openCrossRepoGraph`), letting you trace rationale across repository boundaries without switching contexts.
